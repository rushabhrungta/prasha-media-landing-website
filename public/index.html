About Us

We are a media house that aims to bring, produce and broadcast enriching content that will add to the strength and meaning to lives of people around the world.

Prasha Media is the dream entity of two successful women entrepreneurs Richa Khandelwal Bhat and Vandana Rungta.

FAQs

For Content Creators

What kind of content can be pitched?

We welcome all forms of children's content, be it animation, puppets, live-action, stop motion, etc.

Will I have the rights to my show?

Yes, for shows already created by you, you continue to own that content.

How can I earn from this?

We are happy to revenue-share with you a percentage of revenue from the ads we run on your show.

How long will my content be on Laddoo Play?

Our initial agreement will be for 12 months which can be renewed again.

For sponsors/advertisers

What is the USP of your channel?

We are aiming to impact the young minds of the new generation with thought provoking and constructive content instead of the mind blowing creations being dished out

How does my brand benefit from associating with you?

We are bringing to you a very unique and dynamic segment of viewers i.e kids and parents. This segment had a large retention value, hence will be a huge boost for your brand.

What are the different ways in which we can associate with you?

You can choose to advertise in singular programs or sponsor entire series with us.

Can we get associated only basis of the content bank you have?

We are creators of magic. If you have an idea/script or design of something you wish to create, which resonates with your brand values and ethos, we are super excited to create and broadcast it for you.
