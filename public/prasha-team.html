Richa Khandelwal

Co-founder

Richa Khandelwal Bhat, CEO of Keshava Productions Pvt. Ltd. and Managing Director of Narayana's ArtNirbhar Bharat Foundation, is a celebrated figure in India's creative arts industry. She's recognized as the Social Impact Entrepreneur of the Year and has helmed award-winning television and film productions with international acclaim. Richa's company is empanelled with the National Films Development Corporation, and she has a distinguished portfolio working with major clients like Boeing, Decathlon, ITC, Walmart, Asian Paints, the Gov of Karnataka, Manipal Global, and the Indian Air Force.
